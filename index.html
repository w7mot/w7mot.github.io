QST QST QST DE W7MOT
 
MARCA Technical Ham Net
 
This is a weekly net on the South Mountain 2meter repeater, W7MOT-24.
The Net is open to All Licensed Amateurs and may be received by anyone with a radio.
Check-in with Net Control every Tuesday evening at 8 PM // 0300Z .
This schedule holds except for the 3rd Tuesday when MARCA Members have their monthly club meeting. Instead of a NET join us and have a face to face round table discussion. Our meeting details are found on the www.w7mot.org HOME page ...
Dial in your radio to 443.050 MHz PL 100.0hz and store in memory.
There is no set agenda unless pre-arranged. Check the Website W7MOT.org. Ask a question when checking-in and it will be on the agenda.
Net Control may have topics of interest or "What's Happening" topics.
As you know, MARCA does VE Session Testing and this net can be a great resource to ask the experts.
Want to learn how to run a Net? Ask Net Control if you can give it a try. It's not a 'snap' when every Radio Operator across the globe is listening for a 'newbe Net Control' and are more than willing to break in and help with the learning curve.. No !! that's NOT true, we're really looking for those wanting to take a turn to practice the complex art and skills of Net Control. It is really a fun trip to try controlling a hot topic discussion when only one person at a time can "talk" and every ham can talk on for ever about anything... 
 
"Over"
